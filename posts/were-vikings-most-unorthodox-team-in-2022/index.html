Were the Minnesota Vikings the most unorthodox team in the 2022 season?

football
minnesota
statistics
Author

Avery Peck

Published

April 13, 2023

The Minnesota Vikings may have had one of the craziest seasons of all time last season. To give a brief overview, they won the NFC North, finished with a 13-4 record, and completed the largest comeback in NFL history, defeating the Indianapolis Colts 39-36, after going down 33-0 at halftime.

The million-dollar question that I used different graphs and data from Pro Football Reference to find the answer to is: Were the Minnesota Vikings the most unorthodox team in the NFL last season? My simple answer: yes. It is a miracle they won as many games as they did because based off the numbers they produced; they should have been maybe .500, but definitely not 13-4.

They had one of the worst defenses in the NFL and having Kirk Cousins as their quarterback doesn’t help matters much either.

library(tidyverse)
library(ggrepel)
library(ggalt)
library(ggtext)

teamrecord <- read_csv("teamrecord.csv")
vikingstats <- teamrecord %>% filter(Tm == "Minnesota Vikings")
winners <-  teamrecord %>% filter(W >= 13) %>% filter(Tm != "Minnesota Vikings")

nfloffense <- read_csv("nfloffense")

nfldefense <- read_csv("nfldefense_csv")

ggplot() + 
  geom_point(
    data=teamrecord, 
    aes(x=PF, y=PA, size=W), 
    color="grey", 
    alpha=.4) + 
  geom_point(
    data=vikingstats, 
    aes(x=PF, y=PA, size=W), 
    color="purple") + 
  geom_text_repel(
    data=vikingstats, 
    aes(x=PF, y=PA + 12.5, label=Tm)
  )+ 
  geom_point(
    data=winners, 
    aes(x=PF, y=PA, size=W), 
    color="black",
     alpha=.5) +
  geom_text_repel(
    data=winners, 
    aes(x=PF, y=PA + 12.5, label=Tm) 
  ) +
  labs(
    x="Points Scored", 
    y="Points Allowed", 
    title="The gap between the Vikings and teams with a chance to go far", 
    subtitle="Although Minnesota scored a lot of points, they gave up close to the most in the NFL.", 
    caption="Source: Pro Football Reference | By Avery Peck"
    )+
theme_classic() +
  theme(
     plot.title.position = "plot",
     plot.title=element_text(size=16, face = "bold"),
     plot.subtitle= element_text(size=11)
  )

OFturnovers<- nfloffense %>% select(Tm, TO) %>% rename(OffensiveTurnovers=TO)
Defturnovers<- nfldefense %>% select(Tm, TO) %>%  rename(DefensiveTurnovers=TO)
turnovers <- OFturnovers %>% inner_join(Defturnovers)
vikingturnovers <- turnovers %>% filter(Tm == "Minnesota Vikings")

ggplot() + 
  geom_dumbbell(
    data=turnovers, 
    aes(y=reorder(Tm, DefensiveTurnovers), x=DefensiveTurnovers, xend=OffensiveTurnovers),
    size = 2,
    colour = "grey",
    colour_x = "#013220",
